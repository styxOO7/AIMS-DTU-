About Us

Gathering all the enthusiasts of DTU!

Are you a fellow machine learning enthusiast? Are you intrigued by the possibility of creating machines that mimic the human brain? Look no further because we introduce you to AIMS, DTU-The official society for Artificial intelligence and Machine Learning. As a society we will not only help in building your technical skills but also in developing you as a whole. You will be working with a team of hand-picked seniors and your fellow students on some amazing projects, participate in competitions and get insights from well-placed seniors and, most importantly, be a part of a family!
